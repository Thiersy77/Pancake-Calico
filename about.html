About Pancake Calico Photography  

“Nothing is ever really lost to us as long as we remember it.” ~ L.M. Montgomery

Our Studio is based in Oak Harbor and we are a team of photographers with select specialties.      

Our goal is to create authentic, natural images that best represent you and your family. Each session is a unique and personalized experience that we pour our heart into. All are accepted and welcome here. However you define your family, whomever you love, We welcome you to our photography family and support you. 

We look forward to work with you and help you tell your story!
